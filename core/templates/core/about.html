About DocSeva

A cleaner way to manage important citizen documents.

DocSeva is built for people who want their documents organized, searchable, and ready when renewals, appointments, or emergency situations happen.

Privacy-first design

Documents stay private by default. Public sharing is explicit, token-based, and can be disabled from the document detail page.

Renewal awareness

Expiry dates, renewal notes, and renewal checklists help prevent last-minute document problems.

Role-separated portal

Normal users, admins, and super admins have separate workflows so operations stay understandable and controlled.

What this website includes

The app is deploy-ready for a Django production environment. It includes static-file handling, environment-based settings, database migrations, Docker deployment support, Render blueprint files, security headers, and a test suite.

Document vault

Upload, filter, download, delete.

Emergency QR

Controlled public medical info.

Gov Hub demo

ABHA and appointment mock flows.

Admin portal

Users, tickets, logs, documents.
